Security policy

I take the security of my systems and data seriously. If you discover a security issue, I appreciate your assistance in responsibly disclosing it to me.

Report the issue: Please report any security vulnerabilities you discover by reaching out to me at the email in security.txt. Include detailed information about the issue, including steps to reproduce it.

Don't disclose publicly: To protect users, I request that you do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until I have had an opportunity to address it.

Provide details: Help me by providing as much information as possible about the vulnerability. This may include code snippets, screenshots, and any other relevant details.

No disruptions: Please do not engage in activities that could disrupt services or compromise user data. Use only the necessary steps to demonstrate the vulnerability.

My response: I will acknowledge your report within 48 hours and provide updates as I work to address the issue. I may contact you for further information or clarification.

Recognition: If you are the first to report a valid security issue and do not disclose it publicly before I address it, I will acknowledge your contribution in the repos. I wish I could do a bug bounty but I'm not that well-off.

Thank you for helping keep my systems and data safe!

Please note that this policy was prepared with the help of Microsoft Copilot. However, this policy still stands.
